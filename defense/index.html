The Daily Defender Mask

Our Defense Mask is for every day wear. This stylish face mask comes in many different prints and patterns and can be used anywhere to keep you safe commuting to work, at the grocery store or wherever required.

Make sure to select which layer of protection you would like. Level I is lower protection, higher airflow (recommended for when you're out running errands). Level II is extremely protective (almost medical grade) but the focus is on your protection, not keeping you cool. This level is best used for doctor's appointments, riding public transportation, or going to very crowded places (like a protest).
